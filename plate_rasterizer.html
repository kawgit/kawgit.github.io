Rasterizer

The above video shows my project
rendering the image of a teapot rotating
while the light-source rotates as well.

I developed a webpage that displays 3D renderings of models from various “.obj” files, which describes some 3D model by listing the 3d coordinates of every triangular face which makes it up. My program orders these faces by their distance from the camera, then draws each of them on the screen one by one to create the 3D image of that model.

Here’s a link to the page if you want to mess around with it: https://kawgit.github.io/rasterizer.
